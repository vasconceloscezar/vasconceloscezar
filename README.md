### About Me

I'm Cezar Vasconcelos, CTO & co-founder of **Namastex Labs**. I build the infrastructure that makes AI systems work in production — multi-agent orchestration, omnichannel platforms, data pipelines, and the tooling that ties them together.

Currently shipping the **Automagik Suite** and **personal-etl**, my personal life OS built entirely on agents and structured data.
